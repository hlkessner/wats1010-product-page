Who will win the battle for the Emerald City?

Battle for the Emerald city is the upcoming split album by Substratum and Skeletor. These two heavy hitters of the Seattle Heavy Metal scene dual for the top spot. Skelator, who have been playing together for 15 years, are the vetrants, but will they been upstaged by the newcommers, Substratum?
